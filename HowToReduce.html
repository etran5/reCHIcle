Tips to Reduce Waste

1. Get to know the rules of recycling.
Do a little research and find out the specifics rules for your area, this is very important because recycling standards vary from place to place.
2. Ditch the plastic bags.
Use your own cloth bags to pack up your items instead of relying on your supermarket's plastic bags. Always leave them in the trunk of your car so you won't forget them!
3. Make a meal plan.
In America, food waste is a huge problem. Maybe try only buying groceries that you know you will eat. It may surprise you how much food is thrown into the garbage.
4. Start relying on reusable containers.
Store food properly, this will not only extend its life but also keep things fresh for as long as possible.
6. Learn to repair rather than discard.
Instead of buying loads of cheap, disposable stuff, buy a few high-quality items and repair them when necessary.
7. Cancel unnecessary mail.
If you cancel unnecessary mail the amount of paper waste you produce will be cut significantly.
8. Stop using disposable plates and Utensils.
